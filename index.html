Fantasy Name Generator for Writers & RPG Players

This fantasy name generator creates unique names for characters, cities, magical artifacts, factions, and mythical creatures. Whether you're building a Dungeons & Dragons world, writing a fantasy novel, or designing a game universe, these procedurally generated names help bring your world to life.

How This Fantasy Name Generator Works

Names are built using cultural sound patterns inspired by elven, dwarven, orcish, draconic, celestial, and shadow realms. Each result is created through structured syllable combinations and fantasy-style mutations to feel immersive, ancient, and believable.
